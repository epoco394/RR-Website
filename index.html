Meet The Creator! And Snack Director

I'm Emma, and the fuzzball you see with me is Taz. He is my emotional support, and often times my emotional crisis, while I navigate through life. I've got a full schedule between Taz, driving a school bus, working out, and doing the basic things to survive.

This podcast was born of the desire to do my favorite thing. Share information with whoever is willing to listen. It might be informative, inspirational, you may pee your pants laughing, or you may be downright astounded by what I say. Don't worry, sometimes I am too.I hope you enjoy listening to my clustered railyard of thoughts as much as I enjoy weaving through it and being able to share it with those that understand and find a likeness to it.

P.S. I designed this website myself! Aside from being a school bus driver I am currently in a course to learn website coding. How exciting is that?
